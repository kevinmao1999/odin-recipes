Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Lasanga sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Aromatic vegetables
  • Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. Soak lasagna sheets in room temperature water.
  3. Cook vegetables in some oil until soft.
  4. Add ground beef to vegetables and cook until no longer pink.
  5. Add tomato sauce to vegetables and let simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. In a cooking tray, add sauce to the bottom.
  7. Then, add pasta, sauce, and cheese.
  8. Repeat this last step until the tray is full.
  9. Cook in oven for 30 minutes.
