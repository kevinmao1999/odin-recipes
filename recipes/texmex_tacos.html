Tex-mex tacos

Ingredients:

  • Soft/hard tortillas
  • Ground beef
  • Taco spices
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Sour cream

Instructions:

  1. Brown ground beef in a heated pan along with taco spices.
  2. Chop lettuce and tomatoes into small bits.
  3. Assemble tortillas with ground beef at the bottom, topped with lettuce, tomato, cheese, and sour cream as desired.
